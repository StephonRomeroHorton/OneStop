Our mission is to be what veterans need us to be. Whether that is an immediate need for resources, a therapeutic outlet or a long term goal oriented program; we aim to be a one stop destination for military, veterans and their families.

Veteran Resources

With both urban outreach dedicated to helping veterans in need; and a beautiful retreat home nestled in Michigan's Irish Hills, we aim to be a resource as well as a therapeutic outlet for military service members and their families to enjoy a natural, safe, inclusive,\ and recreational experience.

Who We Serve

We welcome and serve ALL Veterans, Military Service Members, and their Families. Any era, any branch. Any disabilities, any wounds (visible and not). Whether you're in the beginning of your healing process or at the end, we are here for YOU, the VETERAN, and welcome you with open arms!
